BEACON

DISTRACTION FREE LIVING.

INTERACT

Designed to mimick similar clock interactions but bring them up-to-date. All you need to do to interact with Beacon is rotate the outershell, or press the face. Simple hey?

ALERTS

Beacon is designed to fit into any environment whilst bringing you right up-to-date with any alerts. From email notifications to the weather, Beacon brings them all straight to your table top.

CONNECTED

Beacon can be connected to a load of your favourite apps, from Spotify through to Slack meaning you can spend more time away from your phone and more time in the real world.
